Privacy & data

The Hive is a school-aligned college-prep tool. Account data (name, role, email, and content you save such as college lists and tasks) is stored securely so your dashboard can load on any device.

Who sees what: Students own their workspace. Parents see a read-only view of their linked student. Counselors see students who list their email and items those students share (e.g. meeting requests, transcripts they upload).

Education records: If your school treats certain items as education records, use The Hive in line with your district’s policies and FERPA. This app does not replace official transcripts or school systems.

Analytics: We may count which sections you open to improve the product. Counts are stored in your browser; optional server logs require a database table you control.

Questions? Contact your counselor or school technology office.
